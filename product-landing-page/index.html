About game

Hi! My name is Nikita Sazonov and I would like to tell you about the game which I work on. This is the strategy game about the management of people, processes and business development.
You will feel how to be a top-manager of big corporation, how it is important to work with your staff and develop the people and how much your success depends on them.
I've worked for 10 years for different big companies on different positions, so I will try to put everything I faced with, or even more in my game. Please subscribe on my channel to stay tuned.
